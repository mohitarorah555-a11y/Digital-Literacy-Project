# Social Media Do's and Don'ts Checklist

This guide outlines essential practices for responsible social media use as a college student.

## ✅ Do's

1.  **Use Privacy Settings:** Regularly review and use privacy controls to manage who can see your posts and personal details.
2.  **Think Before You Post:** Consider how your content might be perceived by future employers or academic institutions.
3.  **Engage Respectfully:** Maintain a professional and constructive tone, even during disagreements.
4.  **Credit Sources:** Always give credit to original creators when sharing images, ideas, or content.
5.  **Build Your Professional Brand:** Use platforms like LinkedIn to showcase your skills, projects, and connect with mentors.

## ❌ Don'ts

1.  **Share Sensitive Info:** Never post passwords, addresses, phone numbers, or financial details.
2.  **Post While Angry:** Avoid making impulsive posts when emotional; they can be difficult to retract.
3.  **Engage in Cyberbullying:** Never use social media to harass, demean, or threaten others.
4.  **Share Unverified Content:** Do not contribute to the spread of misinformation or rumors.
5.  **Complain About University/Work:** Avoid venting frustration about professors, classes, or colleagues online.
