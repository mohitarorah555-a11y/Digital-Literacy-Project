# Task 5: Cybercrime Awareness Case Study
**Topic:** UPI and Online Payment Fraud 

### 1. Description of the Crime
UPI (Unified Payments Interface) fraud is a type of financial cybercrime where attackers trick victims into authorizing unauthorized transactions. 

### 2. How it Happens (Step-by-Step)
1. **The Hook:** The attacker sends a "Collect Request" to the student via a UPI app, often disguised as a "cashback" or "refund" notification.
2. **The Deception:** The victim is told they need to enter their UPI PIN to "receive" the money.
3. **The Theft:** Once the PIN is entered, the transaction is authorized, and money is instantly deducted from the victim's bank account instead of being credited.

### 3. Target Audience
College students in India are frequently targeted due to their high usage of digital payment apps for canteen and campus transactions.

### 4. Consequences
The immediate consequence is financial loss. Secondary impacts include loss of trust in digital platforms and potential identity theft if the attacker gains further access to linked accounts.
