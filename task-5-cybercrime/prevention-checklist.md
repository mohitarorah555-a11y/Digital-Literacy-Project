# Stay Safe Online: Student Prevention Checklist

1. **UPI Safety:** Never enter your UPI PIN to *receive* money; PINs are only required for sending or checking balances.
2. **Verify Requests:** Always double-check the name of the requester before approving any "Collect" request on payment apps.
3. **Official Portals:** Only use the official National Cyber Crime Portal (cybercrime.gov.in) to report incidents.
4. **Emergency Helpline:** Save the 24x7 helpline number **1930** in your contacts for immediate reporting.
5. **MFA Enablement:** Enable Multi-Factor Authentication on your college email and GitHub accounts.
6. **Link Caution:** Do not click on "shortened URLs" sent via SMS or WhatsApp claiming to offer free rewards.
7. **Secure Connections:** Avoid performing financial transactions over public or unprotected campus Wi-Fi.
8. **App Permissions:** Regularly check your mobile app permissions and revoke access to your contacts or location for unnecessary apps.
